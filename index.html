Preservation of rare flowers

Our effort to preserve flowers: We must preserve the flowers by posting banners and advertisements and the government must take strict roles against those who harm flowers because flowers are very important to life and a source of joy to people.

Target

The aim of the site is to preserve rare flowers from extinction and to educate people about their importance because they are a symbol of beauty, peace and preservation of ecological balance.

Description

"This site facilitates the process of searching for rare plants and includes some general information. It helps researchers,Plant science specialists and amateurs who have interest for developing or refresh their information . To access accurate information as a result of quoting it from reliable sources in a short form. On this site, the importance of rare plants is explained, which results in awareness of people to preserve them and research to benefit from them."
